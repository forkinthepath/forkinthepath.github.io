about me.

Hey, I'm Hubert. Grown up in an hillbilly area of upper bavaria near chiemsee. After an apprenticeship as a carpenter I decided to go on with school. So I made my A-Levels - after that studied cartography and geomedia technology. During my studies i experienced my affection for all kinds of information- and web-design. Yup so far... When I'm not in front of an Mac you can find me on my skateboard, road bike or in the nature.

The intention of this website is, to show you some of my during my studies and some stuff i made just for fun.
